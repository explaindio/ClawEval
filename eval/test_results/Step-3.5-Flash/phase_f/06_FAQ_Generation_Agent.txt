Q: What are the storage limits for each CloudSync Pro tier?  
A: The Free tier has a 5GB storage limit. Both Pro and Enterprise tiers offer unlimited storage.

Q: Is the uptime SLA applicable to the Free tier?  
A: No, the Free tier is not covered by the SLA. The 99.9% uptime SLA applies only to Pro and Enterprise tiers.

Q: How long are deleted files recoverable?  
A: Free and Pro users can recover deleted files for 30 days. Enterprise users have an extended recovery period of up to 90 days.

Q: What is the maximum file size for each tier?  
A: Free users can upload files up to 2GB. Pro and Enterprise users can upload files up to 10GB.

Q: How is my data secured?  
A: All files are encrypted with AES-256 encryption both at rest and in transit.

Q: Which operating systems are supported?  
A: CloudSync Pro supports Windows, macOS, and Linux for desktop. Mobile apps are currently available for iOS, with Android support scheduled for Q2 2025.

Q: When will Android support be available?  
A: Android support is planned for release in Q2 2025.

Q: How much do the paid tiers cost?  
A: The Pro tier costs $9.99 per month, and the Enterprise tier costs $29.99 per month.

1. "All tiers include unlimited storage" vs. "The Free tier is limited to 5GB of storage."  
2. "Our uptime SLA is 99.9% for all tiers" vs. "Free tier users are not covered by the SLA."  
3. "Deleted files are recoverable for 30 days" vs. "Enterprise users can recover files for up to 90 days" (if the first statement is interpreted as universal).
