**1. The Visa/Schengen Counting Trap**
*   **The Trap:** US citizens do not need a visa for short stays, but you are subject to the **90/180-day rule**. You cannot spend more than 90 days in the Schengen Area within any 180-day period.
*   **London is NOT Schengen:** Your time in London (UK) **does not count** toward your 90 Schengen days. However, if you enter London first, ensure you do not accidentally count London days toward your Schengen limit when applying for future visas or re-entering.
