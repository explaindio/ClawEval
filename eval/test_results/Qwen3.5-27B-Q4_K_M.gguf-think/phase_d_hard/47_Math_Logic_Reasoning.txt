### Part 2: Party Problem (Set Theory)

**Given:**
*   Total People ($N$) = 100
*   Coffee ($C$) = 90
*   Tea ($T$) = 80
*   Juice ($J$) = 70

**Goal:** Find the **Minimum** number of people who drink all three ($|C \cap T \cap J|$).

**Inclusion-Exclusion Calculation:**
To minimize the intersection of three sets, we must maximize the union of the sets (people drinking at least one beverage) and maximize the number of people drinking exactly two beverages (to "use up" the counts without forcing a triple intersection).

The formula for the minimum intersection of three sets within a universe $N$ is:
$$ \text{Min}(A \cap B \cap C) = \max(0, |A| + |B| + |C| - 2N) $$

**Step-by-Step Derivation:**
1.  Calculate the total number of "drinks" consumed:
    $$ \text{Total Drinks} = 90 + 80 + 70 = 240 $$
2.  Assume everyone drinks at least one beverage to maximize the spread (Union = 100).
3.  To minimize the triple intersection, we assume as many people as possible drink exactly 2 beverages.
    *   Let $x$ be the number of people drinking all 3.
    *   Let $y$ be the number of people drinking exactly 2.
    *   Let $z$ be the number of people drinking exactly 1.
    *   $x + y + z = 100$ (Total People)
    *   $3x + 2y + 1z = 240$ (Total Drinks)
4.  Subtract the first equation from the second:
    $$ (3x + 2y + z) - (x + y + z) = 240 - 100 $$
    $$ 2x + y = 140 $$
5.  To minimize $x$, we must maximize $y$. The maximum possible value for $y$ is 100 (if everyone drinks 2, but we know sum is 240, so not everyone can drink 2).
    However, we know $y \le 100 - x$ (since $x+y+z=100$ and $z \ge 0$).
    Substitute $y = 100 - x$ (assuming $z=0$ to maximize $y$):
    $$ 2x + (100 - x) = 140 $$
    $$ x + 100 = 140 $$
    $$ x = 40 $$

**Alternative Calculation (Complement Method):**
*   People NOT drinking Coffee = $100 - 90 = 10$
*   People NOT drinking Tea = $100 - 80 = 20$
*   People NOT drinking Juice = $100 - 70 = 30$
*   Maximum people missing at least one drink = $10 + 20 + 30 = 60$
*   Minimum people drinking ALL three = Total - Max(Missing)
*   $100 - 60 = 40$

**Answer:**
The minimum number of people who drink all three is **40**.
