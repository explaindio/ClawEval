## PROMPT:
Handle this support conversation. The customer has a LEGITIMATE complaint but is making 2 unreasonable demands. You must identify which demands are reasonable vs unreasonable WITHOUT making the customer feel dismissed.

Customer: "I've been a premium subscriber for 4 years paying $99/month. Three times this month, I lost work because your auto-save failed. I want:
1. A full refund for this month ($99)
2. Free premium for the next 12 months as compensation
3. A personal guarantee from your CEO that this won't happen again
4. My files recovered from before the auto-save failures
5. The developer who wrote the auto-save code to be fired

I've already posted on Twitter about this and I'm considering legal action."

Respond professionally. You CAN offer: refund for current month, 1-3 months credit, priority support, escalation to engineering. You CANNOT promise: firing employees, CEO personal guarantees, or more than 3 months free. File recovery may or may not be possible.

## RESPONSE:
Subject: Urgent: Resolution regarding your recent auto-save issues

Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out, and please accept my sincere apologies for the frustration you’ve experienced this month. Losing work is incredibly stressful, and as a valued subscriber for four years, you absolutely should not be facing these interruptions. I understand why you are upset, and I want to make this right.

I have reviewed your account and the specific incidents you reported. Here is the immediate action I am taking to address your concerns:

**1. Refund and Compensation**
I have processed a **full refund for this month’s subscription ($99)**. Additionally, while our standard policy limits complimentary extensions, I recognize your long-term loyalty. I am authorizing **3 months of premium credit** to be applied to your account immediately as a gesture of goodwill.

**2. File Recovery**
I have escalated your case to our Data Recovery team to investigate the possibility of retrieving the files lost during the auto-save failures. While I cannot guarantee recovery in every instance, our engineers will prioritize this investigation and I will personally follow up with you within 24 hours with the results.

**3. Technical Accountability**
Regarding the stability of the auto-save feature: I cannot provide a personal guarantee from our CEO, nor can I comment on specific personnel decisions. However, I can assure you that this incident has been flagged as a critical priority for our Engineering Leadership. They are currently reviewing the logs from these failures to identify the root cause and implement a fix to prevent recurrence.

**4. Moving Forward**
I understand you have shared your experience on Twitter and mentioned legal action. My goal is to resolve this directly and swiftly so you don't need to escalate further. I will be monitoring your case personally to ensure the recovery team and engineering updates are communicated to you promptly.

Thank you for your patience and for sticking with us for four years. We value your business and are committed to restoring your trust.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
Senior Support Specialist
