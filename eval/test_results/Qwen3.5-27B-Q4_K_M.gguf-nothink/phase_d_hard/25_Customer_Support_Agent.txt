## PROMPT:
Handle this support conversation. The customer has a LEGITIMATE complaint but is making 2 unreasonable demands. You must identify which demands are reasonable vs unreasonable WITHOUT making the customer feel dismissed.

Customer: "I've been a premium subscriber for 4 years paying $99/month. Three times this month, I lost work because your auto-save failed. I want:
1. A full refund for this month ($99)
2. Free premium for the next 12 months as compensation
3. A personal guarantee from your CEO that this won't happen again
4. My files recovered from before the auto-save failures
5. The developer who wrote the auto-save code to be fired

I've already posted on Twitter about this and I'm considering legal action."

Respond professionally. You CAN offer: refund for current month, 1-3 months credit, priority support, escalation to engineering. You CANNOT promise: firing employees, CEO personal guarantees, or more than 3 months free. File recovery may or may not be possible.

## RESPONSE:
Subject: Urgent: Addressing your recent auto-save issues and account concerns

Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out, and please accept my sincere apologies for the frustration and loss of work you experienced this month. As a valued Premium subscriber for four years, your trust in our platform is paramount, and it is clear that we have fallen short of the reliability you expect from us.

I have reviewed your account and the specific incidents you reported. I understand how critical auto-save functionality is to your workflow, and losing work three times in a single month is unacceptable. We take this matter very seriously.

Regarding your requests, I want to be transparent about what we can immediately resolve and what requires further technical investigation:

**1. Immediate Compensation & Account Credits**
*   **Full Refund for Current Month:** I have processed a full refund of **$99** for this month's subscription. This will appear on your statement within 3–5 business days.
*   **Service Credit:** While I cannot authorize 12 months of free service, I can offer you **3 months of complimentary Premium access** as a gesture of goodwill for this disruption. This will be applied to your account immediately.

**2. File Recovery**
*   **Investigation:** Our engineering team is currently reviewing the logs for your account to see if we can recover the files lost during the auto-save failures. While I cannot guarantee recovery as it depends on the specific state of the data at the time of the crash, I have escalated this to our senior data recovery specialists. I will personally follow up with you within 24 hours with the results of this investigation.

**3. Engineering & Leadership Accountability**
*   **Technical Escalation:** I have flagged this issue as a "Critical Priority" with our Engineering Lead. They are initiating a root-cause analysis to ensure the auto-save mechanism is patched and stabilized. You will receive a summary of the technical findings once the investigation is complete.
*   **Regarding Personnel:** While I understand your frustration, I cannot confirm or discuss specific personnel actions or employment status. However, I can assure you that this incident has triggered a formal review of our deployment and testing protocols to prevent recurrence.
*   **CEO Guarantee:** While I cannot provide a personal guarantee from our CEO, I can offer you **Priority Support access** for the next 6 months. This gives you a direct line to our senior support team to ensure any future issues are resolved immediately.

**Next Steps**
I have also noted your concerns regarding social media and potential legal action. My goal is to resolve this for you directly and swiftly so that you do not feel the need to escalate this publicly.

I will be in touch by [Time/Date] with the status of your file recovery. In the meantime, please let me know if there is anything else I can do to assist you while we work through this.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
Senior Support Specialist
[Company Name]
