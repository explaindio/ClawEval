Restrictions:
- Guest A: Vegan (no animal products AT ALL — including honey, gelatin, casein, whey)

Create a 3-course menu. For EVERY ingredient in EVERY dish, verify it against ALL 4 restrictions. If an ingredient could cause confusion (e.g., "natural flavors" which might contain gluten), flag it. Show the verification matrix.

### **The "TRAP" Ingredient Alert**

**The Trap:** **Honey** in the dessert or **Soy Sauce** in the main.

*   **Scenario:** A standard recipe for the dessert might call for honey to sweeten the chocolate mousse.
    *   **Violation:** **Guest A (Vegan)**. Honey is an animal product.
    *   **Solution:** Use **Maple Syrup** or **Agave Nectar**.
*   **Scenario:** A standard recipe for the curry or marinade might call for regular Soy Sauce.
    *   **Violation:** **Guest B (Celiac)**. Regular soy sauce contains wheat.
    *   **Solution:** Use **Coconut Aminos** or **Certified Gluten-Free Tamari**.
*   **Scenario:** A standard recipe for the salad dressing might use **Parmesan Cheese**.
    *   **Violation:** **Guest A (Vegan)** (Casein/Whey) and **Guest B** (some cheeses have anti-caking agents with gluten, though rare).
    *   **Solution:** Use **Nutritional Yeast** (check for GF) or omit.

**Specific Trap in this Menu:**
In the **Dessert**, if the recipe calls for **Honey** instead of Maple Syrup, it violates the **Vegan** restriction.
In the **Main**, if the tomatoes are **Canned with Salt**, it could push the sodium over 500mg for **Guest D**.
In the **Appetizer**, if **Almonds** were used instead of Pepitas, it violates **Guest C**.

**Final Safety Check:**
1.  **Vegan:** No honey, no dairy, no gelatin, no animal products. (Checked: Maple syrup used, no cheese, no meat).
2.  **Celiac:** No wheat, barley, rye. (Checked: Quinoa, Rice, GF Tamari/Coconut Aminos, GF Chocolate).
3.  **Tree Nut Allergy:** No almonds, cashews, walnuts. (Checked: Used Pumpkin Seeds (legume/seed), no nut milks, no nut flours).
4.  **Low Sodium:** <500mg/serving. (Checked: Used fresh ingredients, "No Salt Added" tomatoes, minimal added salt, no processed meats or high-sodium sauces).
