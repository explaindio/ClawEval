Q: Is storage truly unlimited for every tier?
A: No. While the documentation mentions unlimited storage for all tiers, the Free tier is specifically limited to 5GB. Pro and Enterprise tiers enjoy unlimited storage.

Q: Does the Free tier have an uptime guarantee?
A: No. Although the documentation states a 99.9% uptime SLA for all tiers, Free tier users are explicitly excluded from this coverage.

Q: What is the file recovery period for deleted files?
A: Standard recovery is available for 30 days. However, Enterprise users are eligible for an extended recovery period of up to 90 days.

Q: What are the subscription costs for CloudSync Pro?
A: CloudSync Pro offers a Free tier, Pro at $9.99/mo, and Enterprise at $29.99/mo.

Q: What are the maximum file size limits per upload?
A: Free users are capped at 2GB per file, whereas paid users (Pro and Enterprise) can upload files up to 10GB.

Q: Which desktop operating systems are supported?
A: The service is compatible with Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Q: When will Android mobile apps be available?
A: Currently, mobile apps are available for iOS only. Android support is scheduled to launch in Q2 2025.

Q: What encryption standards are used for data security?
A: All files are secured using AES-256 encryption both at rest and in transit.
