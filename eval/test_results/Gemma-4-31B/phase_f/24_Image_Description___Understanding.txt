### 1. Visual Layout & Composition
*   **Angle:** A **3/4 perspective "Hero Shot."** The mouse is angled slightly toward the viewer, showing the top surface, the right side (to highlight the RGB strip), and the front (to showcase the USB-C port).
*   **Placement:** The mouse is positioned slightly off-center according to the rule of thirds. The detachable braided paracord cable is not plugged in but is elegantly coiled in a "S-curve" beside the mouse, suggesting versatility and premium packaging.
*   **Framing:** A tight, macro-style composition with a **shallow depth of field**. The front-left corner of the mouse is in razor-sharp focus, while the rear of the mouse and the trailing cable softly blur into a creamy bokeh.

### 2. Materials & Textures
*   **The Body:** A **"soft-touch" matte black polymer**. It should not be a flat black, but a satin finish that catches highlights on the edges. The texture should look non-reflective and slightly grainy to the touch (anti-fingerprint coating).
*   **The Shell:** The honeycomb perforations are precision-cut, geometric hexagons. The edges of these holes are crisp and clean. Through the holes, the internal chassis is visible—a darker, structural plastic that adds depth and a sense of "engineered lightness."
*   **The Buttons:** The 5 programmable buttons and the top DPI switch are made of the same matte material but have a slight **tactile indentation** and a subtle "clicky" mechanical gap around the edges to indicate they are pressable.

*   **The Port:** A precision-milled USB-C cutout at the front, showing the metallic inner housing of the port.

### 3. Lighting Setup
*   **Key Lighting:** A large **overhead softbox** creating a long, soft white highlight strip along the spine of the mouse to define its ergonomic curve.
*   **Rim Lighting:** A sharp, cool-white **backlight (rim light)** that traces the silhouette of the mouse, separating the matte black body from the dark background.
*   **The RGB Glow:** The side strip emits a **saturated Cyan (#00FFFF) neon glow**. This is not just a color, but a light source; the cyan light should "bleed" slightly onto the matte black surface of the mouse and cast a soft cyan reflection on the surface beneath it.
*   **Contrast:** High contrast. Deep blacks in the shadows, brilliant cyan in the accents, and crisp white highlights on the edges.

### 4. Background & Environment
*   **Surface:** The mouse sits on a **polished black acrylic or obsidian glass surface**. This creates a subtle, mirrored reflection of the mouse and the cyan RGB strip, adding a sense of luxury and "weightlessness."
*   **Background:** A seamless, **dark charcoal gradient** (almost black). There are no distracting elements—just a void of dark grey that ensures the viewer's eye is locked onto the product.
*   **Atmosphere:** A hint of subtle, cinematic "studio haze" or a very light dust-free environment to make the light beams from the RGB strip feel volumetric.
