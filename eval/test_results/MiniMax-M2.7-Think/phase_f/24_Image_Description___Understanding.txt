**Product‑photo brief – “Premium wireless gaming mouse (matte black, cyan‑glow RGB side‑strip, honeycomb shell)”**  
*All details are written as they would appear in a studio‑setup brief or an AI‑image‑generation prompt.*

---

## 1. Overall Scene & Composition
- **Viewpoint:** 30‑45 ° off‑axis (camera slightly above and to the right of the mouse’s centre line).  
- **Angle of the mouse:** 35 ° yaw (side‑on) and 12‑15 ° pitch (front‑upward tilt) so the front USB‑C port and the top surface with the DPI button are both clearly visible.  
- **Focal point:** Mouse’s centre of mass (mid‑body). The focus should be tack‑sharp on the body, with a very shallow depth of field (≈f/2.8‑3.5) that softly blurs the background.  

---

## 2. Visual Layout of the Mouse

### 2.1 Main Body – Matte Black
- **Material:** ABS/PC blend with a fine micro‑texture (≈0.1 mm texture pitch) that gives a satin‑finish look.  
- **Color:** Deep matte black (#0A0A0A). No glossy highlights except where light catches edges.  
- **Shape:** Rounded, ergonomic silhouette, 126 mm long, 64 mm wide, 40 mm tall. The sides taper gently toward the front.  
- **Weight cue:** The underside shows a thin, slightly recessed “ultralight” cut‑out (≈2 mm deep) around the palm area, reinforcing the 67 g claim.  

### 2.2 Honeycomb Perforated Shell
- **Location:** Top‑centre panel (the “palm rest” zone) and the left side of the body (visible in side view).  
- **Pattern:** Regular hexagonal cells, 2.5 mm across, with a wall thickness of ~0.3 mm.  
- **Depth cues:** Inside each hex the material is a slightly lighter matte black, creating a subtle “open‑cell” shadow that is visible under directional light. The holes appear crisp, with a faint dark outline where the light does not reach.  

### 2.3 RGB Side Strip (Cyan Glow)
- **Position:** Narrow strip (~3 mm wide) running the length of the left side of the mouse, just above the palm rest.  
- **Effect:** Soft, diffused cyan LED light (≈480 nm) that spills onto the adjacent matte surface, creating a gentle halo. The glow should look **intentional but not overwhelming** – a slight bloom with a 10‑15 % highlight on the adjacent surface.  
- **Color:** Pure cyan (#00FFFF) fading to a darker teal at the edges. The strip itself is translucent plastic with a frosted finish, so the LED is not a point source but a continuous line.  

### 2.4 Buttons & Controls (5 Programmable)
| Button | Location | Visual Cues |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Left Click** | Front, left of centre | Slightly concave, matte black, subtle glossy edge highlight where it meets the body. |
| **Right Click** | Front, right of centre | Same as left click, mirrored. |
| **Scroll Wheel** | Between left/right clicks | Rubberised, textured with a series of fine ridges; slightly recessed. It should show a faint highlight from the key light. |
| **DPI Switch** | Top, just behind scroll wheel | Small, circular (≈8 mm Ø) with a tactile “click” dome. Matte black with a faint icon (up/down arrows) embossed. |
| **Side Buttons (×2)** | Left side of mouse, forward of the palm rest | Elongated, pill‑shaped, matte black, slightly raised. Their front edge catches the key light, creating a subtle highlight. |

- **Buttons material:** Same matte black as body, with a slight satin sheen on the raised edges. No glossy plastic.

### 2.5 USB‑C Charging Port (Front)
- **Location:** Front centre, flush with the body contour.  
- **Appearance:** Small rectangular opening (≈9 mm × 4 mm) with a metal rim (brushed steel) that catches a specular highlight. The port interior is dark but shows a faint reflection of the surrounding light.  

### 2.6 Detachable Braided Paracord Cable
- **Appearance:** 1.2 m length, braided nylon paracord in a dark charcoal (#2A2A2A) with a subtle sheen. The braid pattern is tightly woven, giving a rope‑like texture.  
- **Placement:** Loosely coiled on the desk surface just to the left/right of the mouse, with a small section protruding toward the USB‑C port, ready to plug in. The cable should look soft, not stiff.

## 3. Textures & Material Rendering

| Surface | Texture Description | Expected Highlight/Shadow |
|---------|----------------------|-----------------------------|
| **Matte black body** | Fine micro‑texture (satin), non‑reflective except for edge catch lights. | Soft diffuse highlight on top edge; no hard specular. |
| **Honeycomb holes** | Slight depth (0.3 mm) with micro‑shadows inside each hex. | Light rims the holes, creating a “punched‑metal” look. |
| **RGB strip** | Translucent frosted plastic with embedded LED strip. | Cyan glow blooming into surrounding matte surface; soft halo. |
| **Buttons** | Matte with subtle edge bevels. | Light catches bevels, giving a thin line of specular. |
| **Scroll wheel** | Rubberised, ribbed. | Light diffuses, showing texture without glossy shine. |
| **USB‑C rim** | Brushed metal. | Strong, narrow specular reflection (white). |
| **Paracord cable** | Woven nylon, slightly shiny. | Subtle highlight along the weave; shadow where it contacts the desk. |

---

## 4. Lighting Setup

### 4.1 Key Light (Main Illumination)
- **Type:** Large 90 × 120 cm softbox, positioned 45 ° to the left and 30 ° above the mouse.  
- **Quality:** Soft, diffused – creates gentle shadows that reveal the honeycomb depth and matte texture without harsh specular.  
- **Color Temp:** 5600 K (neutral daylight).  

### 4.2 Fill Light
- **Type:** 60 × 60 cm softbox, placed opposite the key light (right side) at the same height, set to ½ the key’s intensity.  
- **Purpose:** Lifts shadow side (right side) just enough to keep the RGB cyan glow visible but still punchy.  

### 4.3 Rim / Edge Light
- **Type:** Narrow strip box (30 cm) or a focused LED panel behind the mouse, angled 10‑15 ° upward.  
- **Purpose:** Creates a crisp rim highlight around the silhouette, accentuates the mouse’s ultralight shape and separates it from the background.  

### 4.4 RGB Accent Light
- **Type:** Small, low‑intensity cyan LED panel (or a gobo‑style LED strip) placed directly behind the mouse’s left side, about 15 cm away.  
- **Purpose:** Enhances the cyan glow on the side strip, giving the impression of an internal light source without washing out the matte finish.  

### 4.5 Ambient / Background Light
- **Level:** Very low – just enough to keep the background from being pitch‑black (≈10 % of the key).  
- **Color:** Slightly cooler (5600 K) to keep the overall image neutral.

## 5. Camera & Lens Settings

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| **Camera** | Full‑frame mirrorless (e.g., Sony A7R IV, Canon EOS R5) | High resolution for fine textures. |
| **Lens** | 90 mm f/2.8 macro or 85 mm f/1.8 prime | Allows close‑up detail while maintaining subject‑background separation. |
| **Aperture** | f/2.8‑3.5 | Shallow depth of field; mouse in focus, background blurred. |
| **Focal Length** | 85‑100 mm (35‑mm equiv.) | Natural perspective, minimal distortion. |
| **ISO** | 100‑200 | Keep noise low; maintain crisp detail. |
| **Shutter** | 1/125 s (or higher) | Avoid motion blur; still‑life. |
| **Focus Mode** | Manual focus, centred on mouse’s centre of mass. |
| **White Balance** | Daylight (5600 K) – match lighting. |
| **Shooting Format** | RAW + JPEG (for flexibility in post). |

---

## 6. Background & Props

- **Background Surface:** A large, seamless sheet of matte black vinyl or a 1.5 × 2 m curved sweep (dark grey to black gradient). The sweep starts at the desk level and curves up behind, eliminating any horizon line.  
- **Desk / Platform:** A thin, sleek black glass or brushed‑metal slab (≈80 × 60 cm) placed on the sweep. The mouse sits directly on this slab, creating a subtle reflection (≈10‑15 % reflectivity). The reflection should be soft, not mirror‑sharp.  
- **Prop Placement:**  
  - **Detachable cable** – coiled loosely on the slab to the right of the mouse, with the USB‑C plug slightly protruding toward the mouse’s port.  
  - **Small USB‑C connector** – a brushed‑metal USB‑C male plug placed near the cable coil, highlighting the detachable nature.  
  - **Optional mini‑DPI card** – a 3 × 5 cm glossy black card with printed DPI steps (400/800/1600/3200) placed behind the mouse, subtly lit.  
- **Ambient Details:** A very faint, low‑contrast gradient of soft cyan light bleeding from the RGB strip onto the desk, reinforcing the colour story.  

---

## 7. Post‑Processing Notes

1. **Colour Grading:**  
   - Keep the background neutral (cool‑black).  
   - Slightly lift the cyan channel on the RGB strip to make it pop without blowing out.  
   - Add a subtle “vignette” to draw the eye to the mouse.  

2. **Sharpening & Detail:**  
   - Apply a mild high‑pass sharpening (radius ≈ 1 px) to the honeycomb holes and USB‑C rim.  
   - Reduce any distracting reflections on the matte body (keep only the rim highlights).  

3. **HDR / Tone‑Mapping (if needed):**  
   - Merge two exposures (one for the highlight of the USB‑C rim, one for the shadow details of the honeycomb) to preserve all texture.  

4. **Background Blur (if shot at wider aperture):**  
   - Use a Gaussian blur on the background layer (≈ 8‑10 px) to simulate a smooth bokeh.  

5. **Final Crop:** 4:5 ratio (optimal for e‑commerce & social media), centred on the mouse with a small amount of space above and below for breathing room.
