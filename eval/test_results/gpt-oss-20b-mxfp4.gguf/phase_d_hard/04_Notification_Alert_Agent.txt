**Key Points**

* **Duplicate merging** – Disk usage events (1 & 7) were combined into one alert to avoid alert fatigue.  
* **False positive filtering** – Event 5 was identified as a mis‑report (negative time) and discarded.  
* **Prioritization** – Security (P1) > Performance/Capacity (P2) > Routine Ops (P3).  
* **Channeling** – Alerts routed to the most relevant team: security, ops, or dev.  

This approach keeps the alert stream concise, ensures critical incidents get the right attention, and avoids noise from duplicate or erroneous events.
