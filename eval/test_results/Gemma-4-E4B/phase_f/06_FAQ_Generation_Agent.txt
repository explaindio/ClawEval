**FAQ Entries**

Q: Does the Free tier truly offer unlimited storage?
A: No. While the service is designed for large capacity, the Free tier is specifically limited to a total storage capacity of 5GB. Paid tiers (Pro and Enterprise) offer unlimited total storage.

Q: If storage is unlimited, why are there maximum file size limits?
A: The term "unlimited storage" refers to your total capacity across all files. However, there is a maximum size limit for any single file. Free users are limited to 2GB per file, while paid users can upload files up to 10GB.

Q: Is the 99.9% uptime SLA guaranteed for all users?
A: No. While the 99.9% uptime Service Level Agreement (SLA) applies to the Pro and Enterprise tiers, Free tier users are not covered by this guarantee.

Q: How long can I recover a deleted file?
A: Standard users can recover deleted files for 30 days. Enterprise users benefit from an extended recovery period of up to 90 days.

Q: Which operating systems does CloudSync Pro support on desktop?
A: CloudSync Pro supports Windows, Mac, and Linux operating systems.

Q: Is the mobile app available for Android devices right now?
A: Currently, mobile apps are only available for iOS. Android support is scheduled to be released in Q2 2025.

Q: What is the cost difference between the Pro and Enterprise tiers?
A: The Pro tier costs $9.99 per month, while the Enterprise tier is available for $29.99 per month.

Q: How is my data protected while stored and while being transferred?
A: All files are protected using AES-256 encryption, ensuring data is secured both at rest (stored) and in transit (being transferred).

1.  **Storage Limit vs. Unlimited Storage:** The documentation states, "All tiers include unlimited storage," but immediately contradicts this by stating, "The Free tier is limited to 5GB of storage."
2.  **SLA Coverage:** The documentation states, "Our uptime SLA is 99.9% for all tiers," but then immediately contradicts this by stating, "Free tier users are not covered by the SLA."
3.  **File Size Limit vs. Unlimited Storage:** The documentation states, "All tiers include unlimited storage," but simultaneously imposes specific maximum file size limits ("Maximum file size is 2GB for Free users and 10GB for paid users"), creating a conflict between total capacity and individual file constraints.
