Q: Does the Free tier really offer unlimited storage?  
A: The claim of “unlimited storage” applies to the paid Pro and Enterprise tiers. The Free tier is limited to 5 GB of storage, so unlimited storage is not available for Free users.

Q: What is the maximum file size I can upload?  
A: Unlimited storage refers to total account capacity, not per‑file limits. Free users can upload files up to 2 GB each, while Pro and Enterprise users can upload files up to 10 GB each.

Q: Is the 99.9% uptime SLA applicable to Free tier users?  
A: The 99.9% uptime SLA covers only the Pro and Enterprise tiers. Free tier users are not covered by this SLA.

A: Deleted files are recoverable for 30 days for all users. Enterprise users enjoy an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.

Q: Are my files encrypted with CloudSync Pro?
