Q: Does CloudSync Pro really provide unlimited storage on all plans?  
A: No. The correct policy is that only the Pro and Enterprise plans include unlimited storage. The Free plan is capped at 5 GB of storage; the statement “all tiers include unlimited storage” was an error.

Q: How does the 5 GB limit on the Free tier work if storage is described as unlimited?  
A: The 5 GB limit is the actual storage allowance for the Free tier. Once you reach that limit you must upgrade to Pro or Enterprise to get additional, unlimited storage.

Q: Does the 99.9 % uptime SLA apply to the Free plan?  
A: The 99.9 % uptime guarantee applies to the Pro and Enterprise tiers only. The Free tier is not covered by the SLA; the phrase “for all tiers” in the documentation was a mistake.

Q: If the Free tier isn’t covered by the SLA, what can I expect for uptime?  
A: Free‑tier users can still use the service, but they may experience occasional downtime without the contractual uptime guarantee that paid users receive.

Q: How long can I recover deleted files, and does this differ by plan?  
A: All users have a standard 30‑day recovery window for deleted files. Enterprise customers can extend this to up to 90 days through an optional feature.
