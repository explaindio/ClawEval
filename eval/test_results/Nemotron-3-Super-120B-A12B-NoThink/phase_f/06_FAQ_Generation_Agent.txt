Q: What storage does the Free tier actually provide?  
A: Although the documentation says all tiers include unlimited storage, the Free tier is limited to 5GB of storage; the unlimited storage claim applies only to the paid Pro and Enterprise tiers.  

Q: Is there an uptime SLA for Free tier users?  
A: The 99.9% uptime SLA applies to Pro and Enterprise tiers; Free tier users are not covered by this SLA.  

Q: Does “unlimited storage” mean there are no file size limits?  
A: Unlimited storage refers to total account capacity (except the Free tier’s 5GB cap). Individual file size limits still apply: 2GB for Free users and 10GB for Pro and Enterprise users.  

Q: Which operating systems are supported by the desktop client?  
A: The service supports Windows, Mac, and Linux desktop clients.  

Q: Are mobile apps available for Android?  
A: Currently, mobile apps are available for iOS only; Android support is planned for Q2 2025.  

Q: How long can I recover deleted files?  
A: Deleted files are recoverable for 30 days for all users; Enterprise users enjoy an extended recovery window of up to 90 days.  

Q: What encryption does CloudSync Pro use?  
A: Files are encrypted with AES-256 both at rest and in transit.  

Q: What are the pricing tiers for CloudSync Pro?  
A: There are three tiers: Free (no cost), Pro at $9.99 per month, and Enterprise at $29.99 per month.
