Q: What storage capacity does the Free tier provide, and why does the documentation sometimes mention unlimited storage?  
A: The Free tier is limited to **5 GB** of total storage. The statement that “all tiers include unlimited storage” was an error; the correct policy is a 5 GB limit for Free, while Pro and Enterprise have effectively unlimited storage.

Q: What are the maximum file sizes I can upload?  
A: Free users can upload files up to **2 GB**; paid (Pro and Enterprise) users can upload files up to **10 GB**.

Q: Which platforms can I use CloudSync Pro on?  
A: Desktop clients are available for **Windows, macOS, and Linux**. Mobile apps currently run on **iOS**, and Android support is planned for **Q2 2025**.

Q: Does the 99.9 % uptime SLA apply to the Free tier?  
A: No. The **99.9 % uptime SLA** is guaranteed for **Pro and Enterprise** tiers. The **Free tier is not covered** by the SLA.

Q: How long are deleted files kept before they are permanently removed?  
A: **Free and Pro** accounts retain deleted files for **30 days**. **Enterprise** accounts can retain deleted files for **up to 90 days**, giving them a longer recovery window.

Q: How can I upgrade from the Free tier to Pro?  
A: Log in to your account, go to **Settings → Subscription**, select **Upgrade**, choose the **Pro** plan, and follow the payment prompts. The new storage and file‑size limits take effect immediately.

Q: How is my data secured?  
A: All files are encrypted with **AES‑256** both **at rest** and **in transit**, ensuring end‑to‑end protection.

Q: How do I recover a file that I accidentally deleted?  
A: Go to the **“Trash”** or **“Deleted Files”** section in the app, locate the file, and click **“Restore.”** If the file is older than the retention period (30 days for Free/Pro, 90 days for Enterprise), it can no longer be recovered.
