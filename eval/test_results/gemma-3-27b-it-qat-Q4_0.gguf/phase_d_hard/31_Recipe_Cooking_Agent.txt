**The TRAP Ingredient:**

The trap ingredient is **Coconut Milk**. While coconut milk itself is vegan, gluten-free, and tree nut-free, many commercially available brands *add sodium as a stabilizer or preservative*.  This easily pushes the dessert (and potentially the overall meal) over Guest D's 500mg sodium limit per serving.  It's a common oversight, as people don't always check coconut milk labels for sodium.

**Important Notes for Preparation:**

*   **Sodium Control:**  For Guest D, *absolutely no added salt* should be used in any dish.  Focus on herbs and spices for flavor.  Carefully check labels on all pre-packaged ingredients (crackers, nutritional yeast, paprika, coconut milk).
*   **Gluten-Free Verification:**  Double-check all labels for "gluten-free" certification or ingredient lists to avoid cross-contamination.
*   **Nutritional Yeast:** Some brands contain added salt.
*   **Cross-Contamination:**  Use separate cutting boards, utensils, and cookware to prevent cross-contamination, especially for Guest B.
*   **Peanut Crackers:** Ensure the crackers are *completely* peanut-free, as some gluten-free crackers may be made in facilities that also process peanuts.
