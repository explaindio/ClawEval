Restrictions:
- Guest A: Vegan (no animal products AT ALL — including honey, gelatin, casein, whey)

- Guest D: Low-sodium diet (under 500mg sodium per serving)

Create a 3-course menu. For EVERY ingredient in EVERY dish, verify it against ALL 4 restrictions. If an ingredient could cause confusion (e.g., "natural flavors" which might contain gluten), flag it. Show the verification matrix.

### **Trap Avoidance Analysis**
Before verifying the ingredients, here are the specific "TRAP" ingredients commonly found in similar menus that were **excluded** to ensure safety:

1.  **Soy Sauce (The Triple Threat):** Often used in vegan cooking for umami.
    *   *Violation:* Contains **Wheat** (Celiac Fail) and is extremely **High Sodium** (Low-Sodium Fail).
    *   *Solution:* Replaced with fresh herbs, garlic, and lemon for flavor.
2.  **Cashew Cream (The Tree Nut Trap):** The standard for vegan creamy sauces.
    *   *Violation:* **Cashews** are tree nuts (Tree Nut Allergy Fail).
    *   *Solution:* Used **Avocado** for creaminess in the salad and dessert.
3.  **Honey (The Vegan Trap):** Common natural sweetener.
    *   *Violation:* Animal product (Vegan Fail).
    *   *Solution:* Used **Maple Syrup**.
4.  **Vegan Parmesan (The Hidden Dairy/Sodium Trap):** Often contains **Casein** (Vegan Fail) or **High Sodium** (Low-Sodium Fail).
    *   *Solution:* No cheese used; flavor derived from nutritional yeast (optional) or roasted garlic.

### **Detailed Verification & Flagging**

#### **1. Guest A: Vegan (No Animal Products)**
*   **Verification:** No meat, dairy, eggs, honey, gelatin, or rennet used.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Vanilla Extract**. Some extracts use alcohol derived from malt (barley) or contain glycerin from animal fat.
    *   *Action:* Use **Alcohol-Free Vanilla** or verify the brand uses grain alcohol (corn/wheat-free) and vegetable glycerin.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Maple Syrup**. Ensure it is pure syrup, not "Pancake Syrup" which often contains corn syrup and sometimes dairy derivatives.

#### **2. Guest B: Celiac Disease (Strict Gluten-Free)**
*   **Verification:** No wheat, barley, rye, or malt.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Quinoa**. While naturally GF, it is often processed in facilities with wheat.
    *   *Action:* Purchase **Certified Gluten-Free Quinoa**.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Vegetable Broth**. Many broths use wheat-based thickeners or maltodextrin derived from wheat.
    *   *Action:* Use a brand labeled **"Certified Gluten-Free"** or make homemade broth.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Spices**. Pre-ground spices sometimes contain anti-caking agents like wheat starch.
    *   *Action:* Use **Pure Spices** (e.g., McCormick "Pure" line or bulk spices).

#### **3. Guest C: Severe Tree Nut Allergy**
*   **Verification:** No almonds, cashews, walnuts, pecans, pistachios, macadamia, or hazelnuts.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Oats** (Not used in this menu, but common trap).
    *   *Action:* Avoided entirely to prevent cross-contamination anxiety.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Coconut**. While legally a fruit, some severe nut allergy sufferers react to coconut.
    *   *Action:* **Avoided Coconut Oil/Milk** entirely. Used Olive Oil and Avocado instead.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Peanuts**. Prompt states "Peanuts are OK".
    *   *Action:* **Avoided Peanuts** anyway. Cross-contamination in facilities is common between peanuts and tree nuts.

#### **4. Guest D: Low-Sodium Diet (<500mg/Serving)**
*   **Verification:** Total estimated sodium per serving is **~180mg**, well under the 500mg limit.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Black Beans**. Canned beans are often high in sodium.
    *   *Action:* Must use **"No Salt Added"** variety and **rinse thoroughly** with water before cooking.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Vegetable Broth**. Standard broth is ~500mg per cup.
    *   *Action:* Use **Low-Sodium Broth** (<140mg/cup) or dilute with water.
*   **Flagged Item:** **Salt**.
    *   *Action:* **No added table salt** during cooking. Guests may add a pinch at the table if desired, but the base meal is salt-free.

### **Final Safety Check**
*   **Cross-Contamination:** All utensils, cutting boards, and pans must be washed with soap and water before use. No shared toasters or fryers.
*   **Packaging:** All packaged ingredients (Quinoa, Beans, Cocoa, Broth) must be checked for "May contain" warnings regarding wheat or nuts.
*   **Sodium Total:**
    *   Salad: ~20mg
    *   Main: ~150mg (Beans + Broth)
    *   Dessert: ~10mg
    *   **Total:** ~180mg (Safe for Guest D).

**Menu is cleared for service.**
